About me

My name is Mateusz Klos and I'm based in Berlin, DE. I am a freelance UX/UI designer with experience as a data analyst. I graduated with a bachelor's degree in Human-Computer Interaction, where I had the opportunity to gain my first foundational knowledge in UX/UI design. I gained a diverse skill set there, ranging from graphic design and information architecture to technical developing of web applications. This last part helps me collaborate better with developers, as I understand how a website is structured.

I completed my master's degree in Big Data Management, where I learned how to work with large datasets. It has enabled me to learn how to conduct UX research prior to initiating the design process. I am currently seeking opportunities to begin working full-time as a designer.
