About me

My name is Mateusz Klos, and I’m based in Berlin. I’m a freelance UX/UI Designer with a background in data analysis. I hold a Bachelor's degree in Human-Computer Interaction from the Jagiellonian University in Cracow, Poland, where I had the opportunity to explore the fundamentals of UX/UI design, information architecture, graphic design and web application development. My technical knowledge usually enables me to communicate effectively with developers.

Later, I earned my Master’s in Big Data Management at the University of Warsaw, where I learned how to work with large datasets, create user-friendly reports, and draw relevant conclusions. This experience gave me many skills essential for conducting effective research before the design process, as well as and presenting insights in a user-friendly way.

Before diving into freelance design, I worked as a web app tester and data analyst. That experience still shapes the way I think about users, products and problems. I'm open to full-time opportunities where I can grow as a designer and keep building great digital experiences.
