bonsai

introduction

Bonsai is a mobile application designed to instantly identify plant species using a smartphone camera. The app aims to provide an intuitive and accurate tool for plant enthusiasts, gardeners, and nature lovers who often struggle to recognize various plants. By leveraging AI-powered image recognition, Bonsai offers quick and reliable identification along with essential care tips, making plant discovery both effortless and enjoyable. This project focuses on enhancing the user experience by simplifying the identification process and helping users care for their plants with confidence.

the problem

Many plant enthusiasts and beginners struggle to identify plants accurately. While there are numerous apps available, most are overloaded with unnecessary features that make them complex and difficult to use. Users want a simple, efficient tool that provides quick and accurate plant identification without distractions. As interest in plants grows, there’s a demand for easy-to-use apps that focus solely on plant recognition.
